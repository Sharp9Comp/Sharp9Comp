Sharp9

What is Sharp9?

Sharp9 is a composition, transcription, and arrangment studio developed by NoteSax.

What does Sharp9 do?

Sharp9 composes, transcribes, and arranges music, specializing in instrumental and jazz music. Allowing people to request custom music free of charge.*
